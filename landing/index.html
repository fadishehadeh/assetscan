EVERYTHING YOU NEED

Powerful features.
Simple to use.

From small offices to large enterprises AssetScan scales with your needs and keeps your team in control.

QR & Barcode Scanning

Scan any asset instantly using your phone camera. Auto-generates QR labels for every asset. Works on iOS and Android without any app install.

Maintenance Scheduling

Schedule recurring maintenance, get email alerts before due dates, and log all service history against each asset automatically.

Depreciation Tracking

Straight-line or reducing balance depreciation calculated automatically. Always know the current book value of every asset you own.

Checkout & Transfers

Assign assets to employees, track who has what, and log transfers between departments or locations with a full audit trail.

Executive Dashboard

KPI cards, trend charts, department comparisons, and one-click PDF export built for management reporting and board presentations.

Smart Alerts

Email notifications for warranty expiry, end-of-life, overdue maintenance, and pending approvals. Never miss a critical deadline again.

Bulk CSV Import

Migrate from spreadsheets in minutes. Upload thousands of assets at once with our smart CSV importer with field mapping and validation.

Disposal Workflow

Formal disposal requests, admin approvals, and automatic status updates. Keep a clean record of every retired asset for auditing purposes.

Mobile-First & PWA

Install AssetScan on any device like a native app. Full functionality on mobile scan, update, and manage assets from anywhere on the floor.

SIMPLE PROCESS

Up and running
in 3 steps.

No lengthy onboarding. No IT consultant required. Get your assets tracked in less than a day.

1
Import or Add Assets

Upload your existing spreadsheet via CSV bulk import, or add assets one by one. Set categories, locations, and departments right away.

2
Print & Attach QR Labels

AssetScan auto-generates a unique QR code for every asset. Print and stick the label now any team member can scan and pull up full asset details instantly.

3
Manage & Report

Track checkouts, log maintenance, monitor depreciation, and export executive reports all from one dashboard your whole team can access.

MOBILE SCANNING

Scan. Know everything.
In seconds.

No app to download. Open your browser, point your camera at the QR label, and instantly see the full asset profile who has it, when it was last serviced, its current value, and its full history.

Camera-based QR & barcode scanning

Works on any modern smartphone browser. Rear camera auto-focuses. Supports QR codes and common barcode formats.

Public scan page no login required

Share a public QR link so anyone can scan and view basic asset info without needing an account.

Haptic & audio feedback

A beep and vibration confirm a successful scan. Instant result no waiting, no page reload.
